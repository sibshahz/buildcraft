# BuildCraft Redesign Principles: UAE Luxury Identity

## 1. Visual Identity

- **Aura**: Royal, Premium, Warm, Elegant, Hospitality-driven.
- **Tone**: Authoritative yet welcoming. The brand should speak to royal villas, 5-star hotels, and prestigious mosques.
- **Regionality**: Subtle inclusion of Emirati motifs (Arches, Mashrabiya patterns) transformed into modern UI elements.

## 2. Color Palette

- **Background**: #FAF7F2 (Desert Sand/Warm Bone) - replaces clinical #FBFAF5.
- **Hero/Deep Color**: #06392D (Heritage Emerald Green) - evoking a sense of oasis and luxury.
- **Highlight/Text**: #1A1A1A (Deep Charcoal) - for supreme legibility.
- **Accent**: #D4AF37 (Metallic Gold) - used sparingly for luxury indicators and CTAs.
- **Secondary Background**: #FDFCF9 (Mother of Pearl White) - for card sections.

## 3. Typography System

- **Headlines**: `Cormorant Garamond` or `Playfair Display` (Luxury Serif).
  - _Scale_: Grand, "heroic" sizes for major statements.
- **Subheadings & Labels**: `Outfit` or `Montserrat` (Clean Sans-serif).
  - _Letter Spacing_: Extra spacing (tracking) for a premium, spacious feel.
- **Body**: `Outfit` (Modern, clear Sans-serif).

## 4. Layout Style

- **Section Spacing**: Extreme margin and padding (e.g., 10rem+ between major blocks).
- **Imagery**: Cinematic, full-width backgrounds and "Framed" gallery layouts.
- **Flow**: Asymmetrical, organic placements that suggest architectural movement.
- **Dividers**: Geometric patterns or thin gold separators instead of simple borders.

## 5. Image Style

- **Tone**: High-end interior and architectural photography with warm, golden-hour lighting.
- **Perspective**: Low-angle widely framed shots to emphasize scale and luxury.
- **Overlay**: Warm, transparent gradients to ensure white text accessibility.

## 6. Animation Philosophy

- **Motion Type**: Heavy, weighted, and "Silk Smooth."
- **Logic**:
  - _Slow Reveals_: Sections should fade and slide up gently (duration 1.2s+).
  - _Parallax_: Deep background movement to create a sense of scale.
  - _Hover_: Soft-scale or subtle border-glow transitions.
- **Tooling**: Framer Motion.
